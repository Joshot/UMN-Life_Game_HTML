Welcome to UMNLife!

It's all about UMN student simulation

What is UMNLife?

It is a simulation that is specially designed to resemble the life of UMN students. You will experience the daily struggles that UMN students face until graduation.

How to Play?Important!

The current status of your character is shown by each bar. Overtime, the bar will get lower. To increase it click the button next to the bar. Each bar also affects other bars (as seen by the yellow bar), so make sure it's balanced. There's a time jump when you increase activities. You will earn money every minute (in game time) and certain action requires money. Use your money wisely or else you will be bankrupt! In this simulation, you can choose to have a partner but beware having a partner is not always a good choice in college life.You also have to keep studying, otherwise you will get dropped out. The simulation will end if your character is ravenous, is sleep deprived, go broke, or is stressed out. To finish the simulation, you have to keep studying until you pass semester 8, just like in real life.
